Team Introduction

In 2019 (Himachal Pradesh, India), our team named Flipside was formed around PUBG Mobile. It is noteworthy that our team although pursued the carrer in mobile games, also playes many PC games like CSGO, DOTA 2, Apex Legends and plan to participate in their LAN tournaments in near future.

Games

Flipside was formed with a vision to unite the gaming community of India and search for talents who are capable of competing against the world, and also provide them a platfom to showcase and improve their skills. With that in mind, Flipside is an organization which encourages the gamers to pursue their dreams in any genre of games and we promise to help them to the best of our abilities. Currently the games in which our organization is playing are PUBG mobile, Dota 2 and CSGO.

Achievements
